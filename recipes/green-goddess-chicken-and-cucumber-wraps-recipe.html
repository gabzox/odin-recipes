Cheesecake Crepe Roll-Ups

Description

These green goddess chicken and cucubmber wraps are deliciously fresh-tasting. Chicken, avocado, cucumber and mozzarella, dressed with green goddess dressing, fill a spinach tortilla wrap, and then are tied with a green onion leaf or chive for a beautiful presentation.

Ingredients

  • 2 (7-inch) spinach herb tortillas
  • 2 leaf lettuce leaves, washed and dried completely
  • 2 slices mozarella cheese
  • 2/3 cup shredded cooked chicken breast
  • 1 cucumber, peeled
  • 1/2 avocado, thinly sliced
  • 1 thin slice sweet onion, seperated into rings
  • 2 tablespoons purchased geen goddess salad dressing
  • (optional) 2 green onion leaves or 2 chives
  • (optional) grape tomatoes for garnish

Steps

  1. For each wrap, place a tortilla on a flat work surface and cover half the tortilla surface witha piece of leaf lettuce. Top lettuce with 1 mozzarella slice, and distribute 1/3 cups shredded chicken over cheese.
  2. Cut cucumber in half crosswise. Cut one half into 5 spears and set aside. Use a vegetable to shave remaining half into very thin pieces,, avoiding seeds. Place 1/2 of shaved cucumber on top of chicken.
  3. Add half the avocado slices and half the sweet onion rings. Drizzle with 1 tablespoon green goddess dressing. Begin rolling the ortilla from the filled edge of the unfilled edge. Roll wrap in a paper towel to mold. Meanwhile repeat steps with remaining ingredents for second wrap.
  4. Release first wrap from paper towel and tie close with a green onion leave or chive. Release and tie second wrap.
  5. To serve, place wraps on a bed of leaf lettuce. Lettuce leaves may be used for an aoptional outer wrap. Garnish with reserved cucumber spears and grap tomatoes.
